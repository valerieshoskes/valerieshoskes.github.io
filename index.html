About

Valerie Shoskes is a software developer skilled in python/django and .NET frameworks. She is currently seeking employment to start after her expected graduation date of December 2nd, 2016. She is skilled at picking up new techniques quickly and specializes in automated testing.

History

Valerie wrote her first line of code in January 2012. She was employed as a full time software developer in December 2012. After taking some time off for personal health issues, she is preparing to re-enter the software working world by completing a comprehensive boot camp from We Can Code IT in downtown Cleveland.

Hobbies and interests

Outside of coding, Valerie is passionate about preventing animal cruelty and increasing assistance for smaller, less well-known animal shelters. She is also interested in using video games as tools for helping people with learning disabilities pick up skills that are traditionally harder to teach.

Virtual Pet Project

An animal foster care simulator.

A project made after my second week of boot camp. Care for your foster pet to raise their overall wellbeing. If you take good care of them, a "forever home" will adopt them. But be careful! Let any of their statistics reach 0 and your foster license will be revoked!

A Database-First Code Exercise: Wardrobe App HelpMyCloset

Made after my sixth week of boot camp and my first week of MsSQL, HelpMyCloset allows users to view and edit clothing objects in an MVC interface.

The images for the clothes are set in the database and displayed using controller properties and HTML.
